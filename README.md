## Requirements

- **Operating System:** Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher.
- **Internet Connection:** Stable Wi-Fi or mobile data for purchases and activations.
- **Storage:** At least 50MB free space.
- **Permissions:** Internet access, storage for caching, and optional notifications for deals.
- **Region:** Optimized for Nigeria; requires a valid Nigerian phone number for verifications.

**Note:** This app is exclusively for Android users in Nigeria. iOS support is not available at this time.

## Installation

The Edges Network app is distributed as an APK file for direct sideloading on Android devices. This bypasses the Google Play Store for faster updates and broader accessibility.

### Step-by-Step Installation Guide

1. **Download the APK:**
   - Visit the official download link: [Edges Network APK v1.1.3](https://github.com/Edges-Enterprise/Edges-Network-App/releases/download/v1.1.3/Edges-Network.apk).
   - Ensure you download from this trusted source to avoid malware.

2. **Enable Unknown Sources:**
   - Open your device's **Settings** > **Security** (or **Apps & Notifications** > **Special App Access** on newer versions).
   - Toggle **Install Unknown Apps** or **Unknown Sources** to allow installations from your browser (e.g., Chrome).

3. **Install the APK:**
   - Locate the downloaded file in your **Downloads** folder using a file manager.
   - Tap the APK file to start installation.
   - Follow the on-screen prompts and grant necessary permissions.

4. **Launch the App:**
   - Once installed, open **Edges Network** from your app drawer.
   - Grant permissions for internet and notifications when prompted.

5. **Initial Setup:**
   - Sign up or log in with your phone number (OTP verification required).
   - Add a payment method (e.g., bank card or wallet) for seamless transactions.

**Pro Tip:** For visual guidance, watch the installation demo on the official website: [Edges Network Landing Page](https://edges-landing-page.vercel.app/).

**Troubleshooting:**
- If installation fails, clear your browser cache or try downloading via a different network.
- Ensure your device meets the requirements above.

## Usage Guide

Getting started with Edges Network is straightforward. Follow these steps to make your first purchase:

### 1. Account Creation
- Launch the app and tap **Sign Up**.
- Enter your Nigerian phone number and verify via SMS OTP.
- Complete your profile with basic details (optional for personalized deals).

### 2. Browsing and Purchasing Data/Airtime
- From the home screen, select **Data** or **Airtime**.
- Choose your network (MTN, Airtel, etc.) and desired bundle/amount.
- View discounted prices and tap **Buy Now**.
- Enter recipient phone number (or use your own) and confirm payment.

### 3. Making Utility Payments
- Navigate to **Bills & Utilities**.
- Select the service (e.g., Electricity, Cable TV).
- Input your meter/account number and amount.
- Review and pay securely‚Äîreceipt sent via email/SMS.

### 4. Educational Services
- Go to **Education** tab.
- Select exam type (WAEC, NECO, NABTEB).
- Purchase pins at the lowest rates and receive them instantly via SMS/email.

### 5. Managing Transactions
- Check **History** for past purchases.
- Use **Wallet** to top up funds in advance for quicker checkouts.
- Enable notifications for flash sales and new discounts.
